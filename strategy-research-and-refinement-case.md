# Strategy Research and Refinement Case

This case study summarizes one of my recent trading research projects and the process of refining an initial strategy idea into a more selective setup.

## Goal
To explore and validate a simple price action strategy, and to identify the market conditions in which it performs better.

## Terminology
This research uses several Price Action concepts and abbreviated candlestick timeframe terms:

- **FVG (Fair Value Gap)** — a support/resistance zone in Price Action
- **Order Block (OB)** — a candlestick pattern in Price Action
- **4H** — 4-hour candlestick timeframe
- **1H** — 1-hour candlestick timeframe

## Initial Idea
The initial strategy idea was based on the following setup:

**Newest 4H FVG + 1H Order Block**
